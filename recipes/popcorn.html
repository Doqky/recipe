Popcorn

These popcorns are extremely easy to make.

The ingredients required are,

Ingredients

  • Big pot
  • Cooking oil
  • Popcorn
  • Lighter

Steps

  1. Make sure you heat up the pan before spilling cooking oil
  2. Put all the popcorn and leave them for 10 minutes
  3. Wait till the popping sound starts, then leave it for 1 more minute.
  4. Finally, take it out of the pan and fill the popcorn cups with popcorn.
