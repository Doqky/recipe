Lemon mint

This lemon is made with fine lemons, mint, and cold water.

The ingredients required are,

Ingredients

  • Cold Water(Iced)
  • Mint
  • Lemons
  • Glass cup

Steps

  1. Put about 1.5L of cold or iced water in the blender.
  2. Add lemon after you've squeezed it.
  3. Add mint too.
  4. Finally, blend it all together and then p0ur it in the cup.
