Burger

This burger is made with fine beef, lettuce, cheese, and tomato.

The ingredients required are,

Ingredients

  • Supermarket beef
  • Green lettuce
  • Fresh Tomato
  • Yellow cheese

Steps

  1. Make sure you heat up the pan before spilling cooking oil
  2. Place the burgers and leave them for 10 minutes
  3. Flip to the other side and leave them for another 10 minutes
  4. Finally, take it out of the pan and place the bread, tomato, lettuce, and cheese.
